About Me

Hello! I am Aphiwe Andrew Ncayiyane a Full Stack Developer who is crossing into the world of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. I don't just build web apps. I am someone who understands how data flows through systems, how to make sense of it, and how to build intelligent solutions on top of it.

On the development side, I work across the full stack with a variety of technologies. I have experience with React, JavaScript, TypeScript, and HTML/CSS on the front end, and Node.js, Python, C#, and SQL on the back end. I have designed and integrated RESTful APIs, worked with both MySQL and MongoDB, and built production systems that real users depend on daily.

On the data side, I work with Python libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to explore, clean, and visualise data. I can take a raw dataset and turn it into something that actually tells a story, whether that's for a business dashboard, a research report, or an executive presentation. I am also building skills in Machine Learning and AI model development, with a focus on real-world applications like predictive analytics and intelligent automation.

What makes me different is that I sit at the intersection of software engineering and data intelligence. I can build the system and analyse the data inside it. Whether the role is Full Stack Developer, Data Analyst, ML Engineer, or something in between I bring a rare combination of skills that most candidates don't have.

I have worked professionally at Appmaniazar and Capaciti, delivered projects ranging from an AI-powered verification tool, Uvelaphi website, LovedOne APP to a full e-commerce platform, and I am currently completing a Postgraduate Diploma in AI & Data Science at Cape Peninsula University of Technology building on degrees from Walter Sisulu University.

When I am not at my desk, I am usually reading about where AI is heading next, tinkering with a side project, or soaking up Cape Town's energy. I genuinely enjoy what I do and that comes through in the work.
