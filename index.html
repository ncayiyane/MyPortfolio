About Me

Hello! I'm Aphiwe Andrew Ncayiyane, a passionate Full Stack Developer with experience in building responsive and interactive web applications. My journey in web development began with a curiosity for technology and a desire to create user-friendly experiences.

In my work, I focus on front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, along with back-end technologies such as Python, Java, SQL, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB to bring designs to life, ensuring that every project is not only visually appealing but also functional and accessible. I also have expertise in Communication Networks, allowing me to develop robust and efficient networked applications.

I completed my higher education at Walter Sisulu University, where I obtained my National Diploma in Information Technology and Advanced Diploma in ICT (Communication Networks).

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading about design trends, or enjoying outdoor adventures.
